Eclipsed,  
The Sun Glow(e)rs  
Darkly behind  
The puny Moon,  
Who by  
Blocking  
the Giant  
Is gle(e)full  
Despite  
its own  
Caliginos(i)ty

~ Terence Tuhinanshu
